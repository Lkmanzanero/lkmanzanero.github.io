Tourist Spots in Bicol

Mayon Volcano

The iconic perfect cone-shaped volcano is a must-visit for its breathtaking views and adventurous activities.

Cagsawa Ruins

Explore the historic remains of the Cagsawa Church, buried during the eruption of Mayon in 1814.

Caramoan Islands

A paradise for island hoppers and adventure seekers, featuring crystal-clear waters and pristine beaches.

Donsol Whale Shark Interaction

Swim alongside the gentle giants of the sea in this unforgettable whale shark watching experience.

Misibis Bay

A luxury resort where you can enjoy water sports, relax on the beach, and experience top-tier amenities.

Mount Isarog

Perfect for hiking enthusiasts, offering lush rainforests and scenic waterfalls.

Bulusan Lake

Nestled within Bulusan Volcano Natural Park, this serene lake offers kayaking and picturesque views.

Ligao Sunflower Farm

A scenic spot filled with vibrant sunflowers perfect for photos and relaxing strolls.

Daraga Church

A historic church with intricate Baroque architecture that offers a panoramic view of Mayon Volcano.

Lignon Hill

A popular spot for hiking, zip-lining, and getting a panoramic view of Legazpi City and Mayon Volcano.

Vera Falls

A hidden gem surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for swimming and enjoying nature.

Pinangat Festival in Camalig

Celebrate the unique local dish, pinangat, and experience the vibrant festivities of the Camalig region.
